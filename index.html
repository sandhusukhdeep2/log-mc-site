The whole shift, made simple

No mental maths, no spreadsheet. Just the number that matters, right when you need it.

One timer that matters

Every rule, one countdown

While you drive, Log mc shows a single timer — the tightest limit across every work-and-rest rule that applies to you, and the plain-English reason behind it. Tap to rest the moment you need to.

Before you turn the key

Know when you can start

Before a shift, see whether you're ready to roll, how much you can work today, and exactly when your next break and daily cap land — all worked out from the rest you've already banked.

Rest with a plan

See what a break buys back

Pull over and Log mc shows what 15, 30 or 60 minutes — or a full 7-hour reset — earns you in drive time before you stop. It even warns you when a break's too short to count.

Your record, kept straight

An honest logbook

Every work and rest period in a clean, paper-style timeline you can edit, review and export as a PDF — with the locations and odometer readings to back each one up.

A bit of momentum

Stats that keep you rolling

Streaks, lifetime kilometres, hours worked and rested, and a per-shift breakdown — a little well-earned progress for the long haul.

📒

Not an Electronic Work Diary

Log mc is a calculator and assistant. Keep your paper National Driver Work Diary — we help you understand the rules, we don't replace your legal record. Final compliance stays with you and your operator.

🔒

Your data, your iCloud

No ads, no third-party trackers, no servers we can read. Your logbook syncs through your own private iCloud and stays yours.
