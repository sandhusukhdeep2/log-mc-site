Common questions

Is Log mc a certified Electronic Work Diary (EWD)?

No. Log mc is a smart assistant that helps you understand your work and rest against the fatigue rules. It is not an NHVR-approved Electronic Work Diary, and it does not replace your legal record-keeping obligations — keep your paper National Driver Work Diary.

Where is my data stored?

In your own iCloud account. We don't run servers that can read your logbook. See the Privacy Policy for the details.

Why does it ask for my location?

Only to auto-fill the suburb when you start work, take rest, or end a shift — and to estimate distance if you want it. You can always type it in instead, and you can deny the permission entirely; the app still works.

How do I delete my data?

Delete individual entries in the app, or remove the app and clear its iCloud data under Settings → your name → iCloud → Manage Account Storage. Email us if you'd like help.
