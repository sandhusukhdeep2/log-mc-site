Common questions

Is Log Mc a certified electronic work diary (EWD)?

No. Log Mc is a smart assistant to help you understand your work and rest against the fatigue rules. It is not an NHVR-approved Electronic Work Diary, and it does not replace your legal record-keeping obligations.

Where is my data stored?

In your own iCloud account. We don’t run servers that can read your logbook. See the Privacy Policy.

Why does it ask for my location?

Only to auto-fill the suburb when you start work, rest, or end a shift — and to estimate distance if you want it. You can always type it in instead, and you can deny the permission entirely.

How do I delete my data?

Delete individual entries in the app, or remove the app and clear its iCloud data under Settings → your name → iCloud → Manage Account Storage. Email us if you’d like help.
