Get started in ~5 minutes

Join the beta

Open this page on your iPhone, then follow along.

1

Install TestFlight

Get the free TestFlight app from the App Store.

2

Tap to join

Open testflight.apple.com/join/AcHAaD7c on your iPhone → Accept → Install Log Mc.

3

Set up (30 seconds)

Open Log Mc → Sign in with Apple → pick the rule set you drive under (Standard, BFM, WA…) → confirm your home state.

4

Use it on your shift

Start Shift when you begin, tap to rest on breaks, and use the lock button for a big live timer while you drive.

Please remember: this is a test version, and Log Mc is an assistant — not a certified Electronic Work Diary (EWD). Keep using your normal logbook; don't rely on Log Mc for compliance yet. We're testing the numbers and screens with your help.
